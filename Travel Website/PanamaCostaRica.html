Panama

Panama is a country in central America which borders Columbia and Costa Rica. I visited Panama last year as the first stop as I travelled through the Central America Region. It boasts some amazing coastline and even more amazing rainforests. We visited Panama City, San Blas, David, and Bocas Del Toro. Panama city is an up and coming city and the Panama canal is a must see. If you want to get away from it all it is possible to go to the remote islands of San Blas and it is even possible to rent your own island for a night. If you want a relaxing holiday that is in an unspoilt area Panama is the choice.

Costa Rica

Pura Vida! Costa Rica is a country of fun and relaxation. From the beaches to San Jose City to the rainforests of Monteverde, this beautiful country boasts some of the worlds most breathtaking scenery and is becoming the next hottest tourist destination. A must visit is the museums of San Jose which gives an insight into the countries turbulent history. Go to visit the rainforest of Monteverde and spend a day or two strolling around the local mountain villages. This country is inexpensive and throughout the region accommodation and food are very cheap. So if you are looking for a place to go backpacking with little cost, Central America is a great option.
